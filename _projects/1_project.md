**Tackling Challenges in Aerosol–Cloud InterACTIONs with a Novel Framework and New Satellites (ACIaction)**

Hailing Jia (PI), Chenwei Li(PhD student)

Duration: 2025 - 2029

Funded by: NWO Institutes Organisation

[SHOW DETAILS](https://www.nwo.nl/projecten/ozrji30024)

>Aerosols (tiny particles in the air) influence climate cooling by affecting cloud formation: more aerosols lead to more cloud droplets and thus brighter clouds. In this project, we use satellites and models to better map aerosol-cloud interactions (ACI) and their uncertainties. We will use this information to improve current climate models. This project is crucial for making better climate predictions in a world where particulate matter pollution is decreasing.
