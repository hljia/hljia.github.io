This work shows that information on boundary-layer cloud condensation nuclei could reduce hashtag#aerosol forcing uncertainty by about one third.

> **What’s next?** 

>- We need to derive this information accurately from satellites, which is now feasible for 🛰️ SPEXone/PACE and EarthCare. This is also an ongoing work at SRON.
>- Improving modeled anthropogenic aerosol perturbation (e.g., via PPE and data assimilation) is also crucial, as it contributes the second-largest uncertainty in observation-based forcing.

See more details in *[Jia et al. (2026)](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.aea4828)*
